We built AccessFix because most Shopify merchants have no idea their store has accessibility issues — until they get a demand letter.

Accessibility regulations are real, enforcement is growing, and the fixes are usually simpler than you'd think. This blog will cover:

- **WCAG basics** — what the standard actually requires, in plain English
- **Shopify-specific fixes** — how to resolve common issues in the Theme Editor
- **Compliance updates** — new laws, enforcement actions, and what they mean for your store

Stay tuned. We'll be publishing regularly.
