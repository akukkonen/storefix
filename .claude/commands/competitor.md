## Research

Search the web for their website, Shopify App Store listing, pricing, and reviews. Read actual pages — don't guess.

## Analysis structure

### What they do
1-2 sentences. What's their core product and approach?

### Features comparison
| Feature | Them | AccessFix |
|---------|------|-----------|
List concrete features. Be honest — note where they're ahead.

### Pricing
Their plans vs AccessFix ($0 free / $19.99 Fix plan). Who's cheaper? Who offers more per dollar?

### Approach
- Overlay/widget vs real code fixes vs hybrid?
- How do they scan? What engine?
- Shopify-specific instructions or generic?

### Weaknesses
Pull from real review complaints, technical limitations, or compliance gaps. Cite sources.

### Positioning opportunities
- Where AccessFix wins
- Messaging angles to use
- Blog post or landing page ideas to capture their audience

- **Shopify apps:** Accessibly, Accessibility Spark
