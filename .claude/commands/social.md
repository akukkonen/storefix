## Platforms

### LinkedIn (1 post)
- 150-250 words, professional but not stiff
- Lead with a surprising stat or bold claim
- Line breaks between paragraphs for readability
- End with a question to drive comments, or a direct CTA
- 3-5 hashtags: #accessibility #shopify #wcag #ecommerce #EAA

### X / Twitter (2-3 standalone posts)
- Under 280 characters each
- Hook in the first line — stop the scroll
- One stat-based, one opinion-based, one practical tip
- Include [LINK] placeholder
- Max 2 hashtags

### X Thread (optional, only if topic warrants depth)
- 4-6 tweets, numbered (1/5, 2/5, etc.)
- First tweet hooks, last tweet has CTA
- Each tweet must stand alone AND flow as a sequence

## Content angles
- Legal risk stats (4,000+ ADA lawsuits in 2023, EAA enforceable since June 2025)
- "Overlays don't protect you" — courts have ruled them insufficient
- "15% of customers can't use your store" — revenue angle
- Specific accessibility issues merchants don't know they have
- Widgets vs real fixes comparison

## Anti-AI writing rules
- No "🧵 Thread:" openers. No "Here's why this matters:" transitions.
- Don't start every post with a question. Vary the format.
- Be specific — name laws, cite numbers, reference real events.
- Have a point of view. Don't hedge with "might" and "could potentially."
