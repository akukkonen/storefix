What you need to comply with

If you sell to EU customers, these regulations apply to your store.

Accessibility — European Accessibility Act

Your store must be usable by people with disabilities. This means proper image descriptions, readable text contrast, keyboard navigation, and more. The international standard is called WCAG 2.1 AA.

Enforceable now

Price transparency — Omnibus Directive

When you show a discounted price, you must also display the lowest price from the last 30 days. No more inflated "was" prices.

Enforceable now

Cancellation rights — Distance Marketing Directive II

Subscription products must offer a clearly visible cancellation button. No more forcing customers through support tickets to cancel.

June 2026

Common questions

Does AccessFix slow down my store?
No. AccessFix scans your store from our servers — nothing is installed on your storefront, and no scripts are added to your pages. Your store speed is completely unaffected.
Does it work with my theme?
Yes. AccessFix works with any Shopify theme. Fix instructions include specific file paths for Dawn, Horizon, Debut, Refresh, and Sense. For other themes, instructions are generated to match your theme automatically.
What is WCAG and why does it matter?
WCAG (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines) is the international standard for making websites accessible to people with disabilities. The EU's European Accessibility Act requires online stores to meet WCAG 2.1 AA — the level AccessFix scans for. Non-compliance can result in fines and legal action.
I'm not technical. Can I fix the issues myself?
Absolutely. Fix instructions are written in plain English with exact steps for the Shopify Theme Editor. Many issues can be fixed without touching code at all — for example, adding alt text to images is done through the product editor. When code changes are needed, you get the exact code to copy and paste.
Can I use AccessFix alongside an existing accessibility widget?
Yes. Many merchants start with both while they work through their fixes. AccessFix finds the real issues in your theme code so you can fix them permanently. Once your code is clean, you won't need the widget anymore.
What does the free plan include?
The free plan gives you a full scan of up to 20 pages, a compliance score, every issue listed with its severity, and fix instructions for the 3 most impactful issue types. You can see exactly what's wrong and fix the biggest problems without paying anything.
